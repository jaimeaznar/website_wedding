Things to Do

Explore the beautiful medieval towns of Cáceres and Trujillo while you're here for our wedding!

Friday - Cáceres Old Town

Arrive and explore the UNESCO World Heritage medieval city.

Plaza Mayor & Old Town

Start your visit at the historic Plaza Mayor and enter the walled city through the stunning Arco de la Estrella.

Time: 2-3 hours

Location: Cáceres Historic Center

Cost: Free

Torre de Bujaco

Climb this 12th-century tower for panoramic views of the city and surrounding countryside.

Time: 1 hour

Location: Plaza Mayor

Cost: €2.50

Evening Tapas Tour

Sample local Extremadura specialties: Torta del Casar cheese, Iberian ham, and migas cacereñas.

Time: Evening

Location: Calle Pizarro area

Cost: €15-25 per person

Saturday Morning - Cáceres Exploration

Explore more before the wedding celebration.

Medieval Palaces Walk

Discover the noble palaces, historic facades, and medieval architecture of the old quarter.

Time: 2 hours

Location: Historic Center

Cost: Free

Historic Shopping Streets

Browse local shops for crafts, souvenirs, and regional products in charming medieval lanes.

Time: 1-2 hours

Location: Calle Pintores area

Cost: Variable

Co-Cathedral Visit

Explore the stunning Gothic Cathedral and peaceful surrounding alleys before the wedding.

Time: 1 hour

Location: Plaza de Santa María

Cost: €4

Sunday - Trujillo Day Trip

Discover the hometown of conquistadors (45 minutes from Cáceres).

Plaza Mayor de Trujillo

Visit the stunning main square featuring the iconic statue of Francisco Pizarro and historic buildings.

Time: 1-2 hours

Location: Plaza Mayor, Trujillo

Cost: Free

Castillo de Trujillo

Climb to the medieval castle for breathtaking views of the town and surrounding countryside.

Time: 1.5 hours

Location: Castle Hill, Trujillo

Cost: €3

Old Town Lunch & Stroll

Enjoy traditional Extremadura cuisine on a terrace and wander the charming cobbled streets.

Time: 2-3 hours

Location: Trujillo Old Town

Cost: €20-30 per person

Helpful Tips

  • Footwear: Wear comfortable shoes - many streets are cobbled!
  • Timing: Trujillo is 45 minutes from Cáceres by car
  • Parking: Check parking near your accommodation in advance
  • Weather: Bring layers - evenings can be cool
  • Dining: Make reservations for Saturday dinner
  • Language: Basic Spanish phrases are helpful in smaller towns
